What we do

I'm full is an organization dedicated bring light to communities that are struggling with famine. Around the world, more than enough food is produced to feed the global population. Our goal is to educate on world hunger and to bring people together to bring food to the less fortunate. We also organize volunteer meets where we provide food through food drives.

What YOU can do

It takes each one of us working together to fight world hunger. I am full is dedicated to providing avenues for people to donate whatever they can. We have locations all over for you to donate food.
